The Guild

Onslaught first started on Lightning's Blade (US) back in vanilla and has been active every expansion to date. We have since moved to Whisperwind (US) and are looking for more like-minded players to join us in Legion. We like to hang out and have fun while we kill stuff. Come help us kill more stuff and have fun!!!

Raiding

For Legion, our raid nights will be Tuesday and Wednesday from 7:30PM EST to 9:30PM EST. We're a casual raiding guild and we strive for open-minded raiders who are receptive to feedback when it comes to performance. We want everyone to perform their best and are willing to help!

Guild Rules

Code of Conduct

We want to have fun, first and foremost. As such, there is a zero tolerance on drama both in and out of raids. Be respectful of other people's views and beliefs, help others in game rather than hate, and keep the profanity to a minimum. In general, be nice.

Loot

We will be using Personal Loot as our preferred method of loot distribution during guild raids on boss fights. Master Looter will be used during trash pulls to collect crafting materials and BoE items for the guild bank. If you have an issue during the raid about loot, please PM a raid officer.

Attendance

Raids will be available for sign up through the in-game calendar. Be sure to sign up beforehand so we can plan ahead. If you think you'll be late, drop us a line in Discord to let everyone know.

Preparation

Raiders are expected to have the proper gear, gems, and enchantments for their role prior to the start of the raid (If you need help, just ask). Food, flasks, and potions will be provided from the guild bank. If required, be willing to spend time learning character rotations and boss mechanics outside the raid.

Repairs and Services

Guild repairs will be available through the guild bank up to a specified value. Enchantments and crafting services will be available to raiders during non-raid hours at a discounted rate. Keep an eye on our work orders channel in Discord.

Addons

Raiders are expected to have the following addons installed and up-to-date prior to a raid:

  • Deadly Boss Mods
  • Angry Assignments

We've found raids to be easier to run when everyone has these installed. Additional addons may be required on a case by case basis.

Community

When it comes to communicating and socializing during raids and out of game, we use Discord as our voice and text chat application. It's free to sign up and great for keeping everyone connected, even when you're on the go (download the mobile app!). If you happen to be looking to join our raid chat for a raid, look no further.

Recruitment

We are looking for players interested in running Mythic+ dungeons and hopefully Heroic raids for progression. If you are interested in joining Onslaught, read the rules then leave a message in our recruitment channel. Please be sure to include your mainspec/offspec, armory link, and why you think this is the right guild for you. If you have any questions, feel free to message Caelestus and/or Jackfrost in game.
