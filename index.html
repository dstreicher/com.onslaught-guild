The Guild

Onslaught first started on Lightning's Blade (US) back in vanilla and has been active every expansion to date. We have since moved to Whisperwind (US) and are looking for more like-minded players to join us in Legion.

Built on a community of friends who met playing WoW, we value the comradery that comes from raiding together and the perserverance to help each other down that next boss. Our goal is to promote a casual raiding environment that keeps the drama low and the fun high.

Raiding is fun, but we also like to have fun outside of raids too. Whether that may be one of our monthly guild contests (March Movie Madness anyone?) or some achievement hunting, we are always on the lookout for exciting guild events.
